## Overview

Zaratul is a high-sequence Fool-pathway Beyonder and one of Klein's direct competitors on the same pathway.

## Volume 5 Role

Zaratul ambushes Klein after the collapse of George III's mausoleum plan, forcing Klein into a desperate escape through Groselle's Travels.

:::spoiler volume=8

## Volume 8 Role

Klein defeats Zaratul and uses the result as part of his final advance toward The Fool.

:::
