## Overview

Old Kohler is an elderly man from East Borough in Backlund. Klein meets him while working as Sherlock Moriarty.

## Volume 2 Role

Old Kohler helps Klein understand the lives of Backlund's poorest residents. Through him, the Faceless volume keeps attention on people who are usually invisible to nobles, churches, and conspirators.

## Importance

Old Kohler's story gives the volume title another meaning. Faceless is not only Klein's future sequence; it also points to ordinary people whose names and suffering are ignored by the powerful.

## Personality

Old Kohler is weary, practical, and grateful for small forms of stability. His life in East Borough shows how fragile survival can be in the capital.
