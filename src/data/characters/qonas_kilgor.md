## Overview

Qonas Kilgor is a powerful Loen military intelligence figure whose secrets connect the army, the royal family, and illegal Beyonder operations.

## Volume 5 Role

Klein targets Qonas while investigating the truth behind the Great Smog of Backlund. Qonas's death and spirit-channeling expose key parts of George III's apotheosis plan.
