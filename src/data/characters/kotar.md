## Overview

Kotar is a Dark Demonic Wolf known as the **God of Wishes**, a subsidiary god of the Annihilation Demonic Wolf Flegrea from the Second Epoch.

## Volume 6 Role

Klein hunts Kotar in the Forsaken Land of the Gods. After being defeated, Kotar commits suicide to escape, leaving behind a black-velvet "Curtain" of Fool-pathway characteristics that Klein takes as part of his own advancement path.
