## Overview

Ouroboros, also known as the Angel of Fate, is a King of Angels of the Monster pathway serving the True Creator.

## Volume 7 Role

Ouroboros is involved in the pursuit of Will Auceptin and heavily interferes in the matters of destiny and the attempts by various factions to secure the Die of Probability and accommodate the Wheel of Fortune.
