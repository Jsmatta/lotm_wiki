## Overview

Will Auceptin appears to be a child connected to Dr. Aaron Ceres, but his dreams, paper crane, and knowledge reveal a much more significant existence.

## Volume 2 Role

Klein notices Will through Aaron's recurring nightmare and investigates a paper crane associated with him. Will displays authority over luck and fate and warns Klein indirectly about dangers surrounding the Wheel of Fortune Pathway.

## Mystery

Volume 2 does not fully explain Will's identity. He is linked to the title Snake of Mercury and is engaged in a dangerous struggle involving another high-level being of the same pathway.
