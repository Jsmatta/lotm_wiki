## Overview

Ernes Boyar is a Sanguine connected to Emlyn White's Volume 5 mission.

## Volume 5 Role

Emlyn and the Tarot Club carry out a plan against Ernes and send him to Harvest Church. The operation helps Emlyn complete conditions for his advancement to Scarlet Scholar.
