## Overview

Xio Derecha is a small but formidable bounty hunter and Beyonder in Backlund. She is Fors Wall's close friend and partner in many practical investigations.

## Volume 2 Role

Xio operates near Backlund's lower and middle-class conflicts, taking jobs that connect her to informants, criminals, and official channels. Her work makes her a natural bridge between ordinary cases and hidden Beyonder matters.

## Pathway

Xio follows the **Arbiter Pathway**, which emphasizes rules, judgment, physical authority, and the ability to impose order in conflict.

## Personality

Xio is direct, dutiful, and more disciplined than Fors. She takes responsibility seriously and often becomes the practical half of their partnership.
