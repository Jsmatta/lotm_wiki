## Overview

Fors Wall is a novelist and Beyonder in Backlund. She is close friends with Xio Derecha and becomes involved with the Tarot Club during the Backlund arc.

## Volume 2 Role

Fors brings a civilian-facing view of Backlund's Beyonder world. She is knowledgeable enough to recognize danger but often prefers comfort, avoidance, and practical shortcuts when possible.

Her connection to Xio places her near investigations, commissions, and underground information. Her later Tarot Club involvement broadens the club's reach inside Backlund.

## Pathway

Fors belongs to the **Apprentice Pathway**, a pathway associated with doors, travel, escape, and spatial tricks.

## Personality

Fors is witty, lazy when she can afford it, and more courageous than she gives herself credit for. She often complains, but she does not abandon people close to her.
