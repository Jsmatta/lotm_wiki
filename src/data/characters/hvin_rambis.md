## Overview

Hvin Rambis is a high-ranking Psychology Alchemists member and Visionary-pathway Beyonder.

## Volume 4 Role

Hvin's contact with Audrey shows that the Psychology Alchemists are not only a study society; they are embedded in elite politics and have their own dangerous agenda.

:::spoiler volume=5

## Volume 5 Role

Klein kills Hvin under Arianna's concealment and channels his spirit for information about the royal conspiracy.
