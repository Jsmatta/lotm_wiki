## Overview

Bernadette Gustav is the daughter of Emperor Roselle and the powerful figure known as **Queen Mystic**.

## Volume 2

Klein encounters her influence while stealing the Black Emperor Card from the Roselle Memorial Exhibition.

:::spoiler volume=3

## Volume 3

Bernadette remains connected to Cattleya, whom she raised and mentored. She notices Cattleya's contact with a mysterious organization collecting Roselle's diary pages and cautiously investigates the situation.

She also appears during the voyage through the Sea of Ruins, demonstrating power and knowledge far beyond ordinary pirate admirals.

:::
