## Overview

Alice Moretti is a later member of the Moretti family seen after Klein's main story has concluded.

## Volume 8 Role

Ten years after Klein enters slumber, Melissa prays to Mr. Fool with Alice. Alice's brief contact with The Fool's illusory tentacle provides the final quiet family note of the main novel.
