## Overview

Daly Simone is a Nighthawk and Spirit Medium who was close to Dunn Smith and remained involved with the church after the Tingen tragedy.

## Volume 4 Role

Daly joins the final operation against Ince Zangwill. Her participation gives the revenge for Tingen a personal cost and closes the emotional thread left by Dunn's death.
