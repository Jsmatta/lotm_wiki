## Overview

Suah is a high-level entity associated with the Rose School of Thought's Desire faction.

## Volume 5 Role

Suah appears among the powers trying to stop Klein during the mausoleum conflict, tying the Rose School of Thought's high-level forces into George III's apotheosis battlefield.
