## Overview

Old Neil is a veteran civilian member of the Tingen Nighthawks and a **Sequence 9: Mystery Pryer**. He becomes Klein's first teacher in practical mysticism.

## Knowledge

He teaches Klein Hermes, ritual magic, spirit vision, charms, divination tools, and the safe handling of supernatural materials. His humor, thriftiness, and fondness for reimbursement claims make him one of the team's warmest presences.

## Volume 1 Tragedy

Old Neil secretly attempts to contact his deceased fiancée. The hidden influence answering him gradually corrupts his mind and causes him to lose control. His death teaches Klein that knowledge, grief, and seemingly harmless rituals can all become fatal in the Beyonder world.
