## Overview

Amon is the Angel of Time, a terrifying Error-pathway angel whose avatars and theft authority make him one of Klein's worst enemies.

## Volume 4 Role

Amon's name becomes an immediate danger once Klein's circle learns that he may have descended near Backlund. Leonard's parasite, Pallez Zoroast, treats him as a lethal threat.

:::spoiler volume=5

## Volume 5 Role

Amon avatars appear in Backlund through Flora Jacob's loss of control. Klein, Leonard, Pallez, and Will Auceptin cooperate to remove the avatars, but Amon later finds Klein at the end of the volume through a lost marionette.

:::

:::spoiler volume=6

## Volume 6 Role

Amon captures Klein and escorts him through the Forsaken Land of the Gods, forcing Klein into repeated escape attempts. Their journey reveals Chernobyl and the truth that Klein's world is a future Earth rather than a different realm.

:::

:::spoiler volume=8

## Volume 8 Role

Amon invades Sefirah Castle after Klein becomes The Fool. Klein wins the gambit, stripping Amon of his decisive advantages and forcing him to leave Earth.

:::
