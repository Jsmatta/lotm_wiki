## Overview

Pallez Zoroast is an ancient Error-pathway angel living as a parasite within Leonard Mitchell.

## Volume 4 Role

Leonard finally learns the name and nature of the old man inside him. Pallez helps Leonard understand why Klein's survival and the gray fog are dangerous secrets.

:::spoiler volume=5

## Volume 5 Role

Pallez helps Leonard deal with the Amon avatars in Backlund. He remains cautious around the gray fog and treats Amon as a threat that can exploit even small mistakes.

:::

:::spoiler volume=6

## Volume 6 Role

Pallez loses a Sequence 1 characteristic to Amon, but survives. His warnings continue to guide Leonard's choices in the Tarot Club's highest-risk conflicts.

:::
