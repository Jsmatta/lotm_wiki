## Overview

Hermes is an ancient figure tied to the development of mysticism language and Visionary-pathway history.

## Volume 5 Role

Hermes is among the high-level beings involved in the struggle around George III's apotheosis ritual, adding another ancient power to the battle around the mausoleums.
