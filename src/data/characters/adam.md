## Overview

Adam is a King of Angels connected to the Visionary Pathway and one of the most dangerous planners active behind the later Loen conflicts.

## Volume 4 Role

Klein sees Adam's influence behind the Southern Continent confrontation with Ince Zangwill. Adam's presence reframes the revenge plot as part of a much larger arrangement.

:::spoiler volume=5

## Volume 5 Role

Adam's arrangements sit behind the royal apotheosis crisis and Klein's meeting inside Groselle's Travels. By the end of the volume, Klein understands that many separate disasters were being guided toward Adam's desired outcome.

:::

:::spoiler volume=7

## Volume 7 Role

Adam's true position becomes clearer when Klein is trapped in the Corpse Cathedral and learns more about the Visionary Pathway, the Ancient Sun God, and the Second Blasphemy Slate.

:::
