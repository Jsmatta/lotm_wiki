## Overview

Benson is the eldest Moretti sibling and the family's primary provider at the beginning of the novel. Years of office work have made him responsible, patient, and skilled at navigating bureaucracy.

## Family Role

He supports Klein and Melissa on a modest salary and often hides financial pressure behind jokes. When Klein finds work, the family can move from their cramped apartment to a more comfortable home on Daffodil Street.

## Personality

Benson is warm, witty, and deeply protective of his siblings. His ordinary ambitions provide a sharp contrast to the dangerous mystical world surrounding Klein.
