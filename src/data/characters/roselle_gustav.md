## Overview

Roselle Gustav is a transmigrator, former emperor, and central source of hidden history through his diary and legacy.

## Volume 7 Role

Bernadette reaches Roselle's final mausoleum and learns the truth of her father's corruption. Klein meets Roselle directly and gains a clearer picture of the apocalypse-level threats outside Earth.

:::spoiler volume=8

## Volume 8 Role

Roselle's legacy remains part of Klein's final preparations, especially through Bernadette and the Magic Wishing Lamp.
