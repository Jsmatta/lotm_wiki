## Overview

Ludwell is Admiral Hell, a pirate admiral whose authority and equipment are tied to the Death Pathway.

## Volume 4 Role

Azik and Klein board the Black Tulip because Ludwell possesses clues connected to Death. Ludwell is defeated and becomes one of Klein's most important marionettes.
