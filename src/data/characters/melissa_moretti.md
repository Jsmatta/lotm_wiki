## Overview

Melissa is Klein's younger sister and a student at Tingen Technical School. She is talented with machinery, careful with money, and determined to build a stable future for the Moretti family.

## Personality

Practical and direct, Melissa is suspicious when Klein's behavior suddenly changes. Over time she accepts his new confidence and appreciates his efforts to improve the family's life.

## Volume 1 Role

Melissa represents the ordinary life Klein wants to protect. Meals, household expenses, school, and family outings give him reasons to remain grounded while supernatural dangers gather around Tingen.
