## Overview

Senor is **Admiral of Blood**, a pirate admiral and high-level Chained-pathway Beyonder associated with the Rose School of Thought.

## Abilities

As a Wraith, Senor can become spirit-like, possess targets, move through reflective surfaces, and fight with supernatural speed and concealment.

## Volume 3 Role

Senor participates in the Rose School of Thought's attempt to capture Klein near Bayam. Klein survives the battle with help from Reinette Tinekerr and Azik Eggers, then converts Senor into his first major Marionettist puppet.
