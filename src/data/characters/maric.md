## Overview

Maric is Sharron's partner in Backlund and a Beyonder of the Chained Pathway. He is tied to the same dangerous circle of conflicts that surrounds Sharron.

## Volume 2 Role

Maric helps Klein contact Sharron and becomes part of Klein's Backlund network. His knowledge of local Beyonder circles makes him useful when Klein needs information, protection, or discreet help.

## Abilities

Maric commands living corpses and fights with the disturbing resilience associated with the Chained Pathway. His abilities make him effective in ambushes and night operations.

## Personality

Maric is tense, gloomy, and more visibly strained than Sharron. He is still dependable when a deal is made, especially when the matter concerns shared enemies.
