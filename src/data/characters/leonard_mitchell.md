## Overview

Leonard Mitchell is a young member of the Tingen Nighthawks. He has black hair, green eyes, a relaxed manner, and a habit of treating life as though he were the protagonist of a dramatic story.

## Abilities

Leonard follows the Sleepless Pathway and is a **Sequence 8: Midnight Poet** during Volume 1. He is stronger at night, can see in darkness, and uses poetry with supernatural effects to calm or weaken targets.

## Volume 1 Role

Although Leonard often appears casual, he is observant, brave, and suspicious of coincidences. He works closely with Klein during several investigations and senses that both of them possess unusual secrets.

## After Tingen

Leonard survives the final disaster. The loss of his captain and teammates gives him a personal reason to pursue those responsible.

:::spoiler volume=4

## Volume 4

Leonard traces Gehrman Sparrow back through Sherlock Moriarty to Klein Moretti and confirms that Klein survived Tingen. He also learns the name of the parasite inside him: Pallez Zoroast.

:::

:::spoiler volume=5

## Volume 5

Leonard joins the Tarot Club as **The Star** and is promoted to **Sequence 5: Spirit Warlock**. He works with Klein, Pallez, and Will Auceptin to clear Amon avatars from Backlund, then remains one of the main church-side links to Klein during the war and George III crisis.

:::
