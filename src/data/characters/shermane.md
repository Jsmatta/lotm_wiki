## Overview

Shermane is a Demoness-pathway Beyonder drawn into the same political and supernatural trap surrounding Trissy and Viscount Stratford.

## Volume 5 Role

Shermane advances to Witch but loses control during the Stratford incident. Xio kills Shermane, making the investigation into Stratford and Xio's father far more personal.
