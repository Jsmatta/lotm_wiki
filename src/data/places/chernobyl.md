## Overview

Chernobyl is the place where Klein discovers that the world of Lord of Mysteries is a future version of Earth.

## Volume 6 Role

Amon takes Klein through Chernobyl during their journey. The revelation fully changes Klein's understanding of transmigration, history, and Sefirah Castle.
