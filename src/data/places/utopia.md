## Overview

Utopia is the marionette town Klein constructs for his Sequence 1 advancement ritual.

## Volume 7 Role

Klein controls the entire town while hiding his real body. Zaratul, Adam, and Amon's pressures turn Utopia into the stage for Klein's Attendant of Mysteries advancement.
