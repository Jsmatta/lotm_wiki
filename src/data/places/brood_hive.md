## Overview

The Brood Hive is the Sefirah for the Moon and Mother pathways, originally belonging to the Mother Goddess of Depravity.

## Volume 8 Role

It remains a sealed focal point that the Mother Goddess of Depravity continuously attempts to reclaim from beyond the world barrier.
