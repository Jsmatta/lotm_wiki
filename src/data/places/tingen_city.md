## Overview

Tingen is a city in Awwa County of the Loen Kingdom and the main setting of Volume 1. Known as the **City of Universities**, it contains Tingen University, Khoy University, and several specialist colleges.

## Character

The city mixes factories, universities, churches, middle-class streets, and poorer districts. The Khoy and Tussock rivers meet within Tingen, and steam transport connects its boroughs.

## Supernatural Security

Three official Beyonder teams operate in the city:

- The Nighthawks of the Church of the Evernight Goddess
- The Mandated Punishers of the Church of the Lord of Storms
- The Machinery Hivemind of the Church of Steam and Machinery

Klein's family home, Blackthorn Security Company, the Divination Club, and the Tingen Chanis Gate are all located here.
