## Overview

Harvest Church is Father Utravsky's church in Backlund. It becomes important through Emlyn White and Klein's interactions with the local Beyonder community.

## Volume 2 Role

The church is where Klein encounters Emlyn's unusual situation and the influence of Father Utravsky. After the Great Smog, it also becomes a place associated with treatment and relief for the sick.

## Key Figures

- **Father Utravsky** — the priest connected to the church
- **Emlyn White** — a vampire whose story intersects with the church
- **Klein Moretti** — involved through Sherlock Moriarty's investigations
