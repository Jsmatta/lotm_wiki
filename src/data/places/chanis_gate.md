## Overview

A Chanis Gate is a heavily protected underground area beneath major cathedrals of the Church of the Evernight Goddess. Tingen's gate lies below Saint Selena Cathedral.

## Appearance and Purpose

The entrance is a cold black iron double gate carved with seven sacred emblems. Beyond it are secure zones used to store:

- Sealed Artifacts
- Potion formulas
- Beyonder ingredients
- Dangerous supernatural materials
- Captured cultists and corrupted individuals

## Security

Formal Nighthawks guard the entrance in shifts. Church Keepers, traps, seals, and the power of stored artifacts protect the interior. The area becomes especially dangerous at night.
