## Overview

Moon City is another surviving settlement in the Forsaken Land of the Gods.

## Volume 6 Role

Klein encounters Moon City and its people while traveling through the forsaken land. Their survival expands the scope of humanity beyond the City of Silver.

:::spoiler volume=7

## Volume 7 Role

Moon City survivors join the City of Silver's migration to Bayam and the outside world.

:::
