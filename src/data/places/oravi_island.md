## Overview

Oravi Island is a Loen-controlled island where Klein travels while protecting Darkwill and the Die of Probability.

## Volume 3 Role

Klein accepts a separate commission to impersonate Admiral Amyrius Rieveldt for three days. The task tests his Faceless acting by requiring him to reproduce Amyrius's behavior, relationships, and authority under close observation.

The assignment becomes dangerous when a plot connected to the Mother Tree of Desire attempts to corrupt him through Cynthia.
