## Overview

Backlund is the capital of the Loen Kingdom and the main setting of Volume 2: Faceless. Klein arrives there under the identity Sherlock Moriarty.

## Volume 2 Role

Backlund is far larger and more politically complex than Tingen. Its surface includes nobles, clubs, exhibitions, churches, newspapers, and detective work; beneath that surface are intelligence agencies, secret organizations, cults, and Beyonder conspiracies.

## Key Areas

- **Minsk Street** — Sherlock Moriarty's residence during much of the arc
- **East Borough** — the impoverished district most exposed to the city's cruelty
- **Harvest Church** — Father Utravsky's church and an important location for Emlyn White
- **Roselle Memorial Exhibition** — the public event where Klein steals the Black Emperor Card

## Importance

Backlund changes Klein's understanding of the world. He sees that danger does not come only from monsters and cultists; it can also come from institutions, class systems, and powerful people hiding behind respectability.
