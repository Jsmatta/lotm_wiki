## Overview

The Sea of Ruins is a dangerous region of the Sonia Sea filled with abnormal weather, ruins, monsters, and supernatural phenomena.

## Volume 3 Role

Klein sails there aboard Cattleya's ship, the Future, to find mermaids required for his Marionettist promotion ritual.

The voyage includes contact with Bernadette Gustav, Frank Lee's experiments, Anderson Hood, and a dream connected to the Giant King's Court. Ordinary navigation is unreliable, making an experienced pirate crew essential.
