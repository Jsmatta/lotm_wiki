## Overview

The Forsaken Land of the Gods is an isolated, lightless region where the City of Silver and Moon City survived for generations.

## Volume 6 Role

Amon brings Klein into the region. Klein later travels through it independently, reaches the Giant King's Court, and opens the way for the City of Silver to reach the outside world.
