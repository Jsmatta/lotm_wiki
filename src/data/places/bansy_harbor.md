## Overview

Bansy Harbor is an isolated port visited by the White Agate during Klein's journey to Bayam.

## Volume 3 Incident

A storm forces passengers ashore while an old local custom returns in a violent supernatural form. Klein and Danitz encounter corrupted residents, dangerous rituals, and traces of a much older bloodline.

## Importance

The incident reveals that apparently forgotten traditions can preserve genuine supernatural power. The Church of the Lord of Storms later destroys the harbor to contain the threat.
