## Overview

The gray fog is a mysterious spiritual space reached by Klein through the luck enhancement ritual. During Volume 1, its true name and origin remain unknown.

## Appearance

At Klein's wish, the space forms a vast ancient palace with towering pillars, a long bronze table, and high-backed chairs. Crimson stars float within the surrounding fog.

## Volume 1 Properties

- Strengthens Klein's divination
- Blocks or reduces outside interference
- Allows Klein to summon Audrey Hall and Alger Wilson as spiritual projections
- Provides the meeting place of the Tarot Club
- Helps Klein present himself as the mysterious Mr. Fool

The space is powerful, but Klein's control over it is limited and he does not yet understand what it truly is.
