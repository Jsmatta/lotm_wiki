## Overview

George III's Apotheosis Ritual is the hidden ritual King George Augustus III attempts to use to become Sequence 0 of the Black Emperor Pathway.

## Volume 5 Role

The ritual depends on wartime conditions, secret mausoleums, and the royal family's preparations. Klein enters the mausoleum network through Trissy's ritual information, and the resulting angel-level conflict causes the ritual to collapse. George III fails and dies.
