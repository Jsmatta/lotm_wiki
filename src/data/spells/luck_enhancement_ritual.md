## Overview

The luck enhancement ritual is a ceremonial practice Zhou Mingrui obtained before his transmigration. He performs it again as Klein in the hope of returning home.

## Method

The ritual uses four portions of staple food placed in the corners of a room. The practitioner walks four steps counterclockwise in a square while chanting an incantation, then returns to the center and waits with closed eyes.

## Result

Instead of returning Klein home, the ritual brings his spirit into the mysterious space above the gray fog. It also causes his first contact with Audrey Hall and Alger Wilson.

## Warning

Its origin and true mechanism are unknown during Volume 1. Repeating an unexplained ritual is inherently dangerous in the world of mysticism.
