## Overview

Dream divination is a Seer technique for obtaining symbolic answers while asleep. The practitioner focuses on a carefully worded statement, enters a meditative state, and interprets the resulting dream.

## Strengths

Dreams can bypass ordinary assumptions and reveal spiritual clues unavailable through direct investigation. The gray fog can strengthen Klein's divinations and reduce interference.

## Limitations

Results are symbolic, incomplete, and dependent on the wording of the question. Strong beings or mystical forces may conceal information, interfere with the vision, or make divination dangerous.
