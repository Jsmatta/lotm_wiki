## Overview

The All-Black Eye is the Beyonder characteristic left by Rosago, a Marionettist of the Seer Pathway.

## Effects

It contains powerful spiritual corruption and can be used to control spirit-body threads. Klein normally keeps it isolated above the gray fog rather than carrying it directly.

## Volume 2 Role

Klein lends the item to Derrick Berg through the Tarot Club, allowing Derrick to expose an Amon-related problem in the City of Silver.

:::spoiler volume=3

The artifact remains a key ingredient for Klein's eventual promotion to Marionettist.

:::
