## Overview

The Die of Probability is a dangerous artifact connected to the Wheel of Fortune Pathway.

## Effect

Its rolls influence luck and probability. Favorable results can make an unlikely success occur, while bad results can turn ordinary surroundings into a chain of lethal accidents.

## Volume 3 Role

Darkwill hires Gehrman Sparrow to protect him while delivering the die to Oravi Island. Klein survives its influence through caution, divination, and the pressure of his own unusual fate.
