## Overview

The Biological Poison Bottle is a mystical item created from a failed Planter-pathway experiment.

## Effects

It can release different illnesses and poisons over an area, making it useful for covert attacks and extremely dangerous in populated places.

## Volume 2 Role

Klein obtains the bottle in Backlund and studies its origin through divination. Its indiscriminate effects require careful handling and reinforce the danger of biological Beyonder powers.
