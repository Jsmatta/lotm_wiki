## Overview

The Antigonus family's notebook is a dangerous mystical object at the center of Volume 1's conspiracy.

## Influence

The notebook does not merely record information. It subtly affects successive owners, draws people toward particular actions, and spreads disaster while concealing the intelligence behind its influence.

## Tingen Incident

Its trail connects the deaths surrounding Klein's original body, the Secret Order, members of the Aurora Order, and the Nighthawks' investigations. Multiple factions seek it for the knowledge associated with the Antigonus family.

## Danger

Possessing or studying the notebook can attract supernatural attention and manipulate behavior. Its effects illustrate why mystical knowledge and objects must be treated as active threats rather than ordinary evidence.
