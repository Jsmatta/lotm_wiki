## Overview

The Blasphemy Slates contain pathway and high-sequence secrets from the oldest layers of the world.

## Volume 6 Role

The history of the Ancient Sun God and the Forsaken Land of the Gods points toward the Blasphemy Slates as sources of foundational Beyonder knowledge.

:::spoiler volume=7

## Volume 7 Role

Klein reads the Second Blasphemy Slate while trapped by Adam, gaining decisive information about Above the Sequence and the cosmos.
