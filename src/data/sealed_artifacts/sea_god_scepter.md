## Overview

The Sea God Scepter is the core artifact left after Kalvetua's death.

## Abilities

It grants authority over storms, lightning, sea creatures, earthquakes, and the prayers of Sea God believers within its effective region.

## Volume 3 Role

Klein takes the scepter into the gray fog and uses it to answer prayers under Kalvetua's identity. This gives him a network of believers and limited influence across the Rorsted Archipelago.

## Risk

The scepter contains powerful mental corruption. Klein can use it safely only with the protection and isolation provided above the gray fog.
