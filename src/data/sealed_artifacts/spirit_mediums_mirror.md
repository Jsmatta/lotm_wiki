## Overview

The Spirit Medium's Mirror is Sealed Artifact **3-0217**, a dangerous object used by the Tingen Nighthawks during the investigation of Madam Sharon.

## Power

The mirror can reveal spiritual traces and hidden supernatural influences. It is useful when investigating ghosts, curses, and spirit-related targets.

## Negative Effect

Its reflection can unexpectedly change and draw a nearby person's consciousness or spirit into the mirror. The artifact must be handled according to strict procedures even during an active operation.

## Volume 1 Role

Kenley White carries the mirror during the confrontation with Madam Sharon. Its dangerous side effect contributes directly to his death, demonstrating that even a Grade 3 artifact can be lethal.
