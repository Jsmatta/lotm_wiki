## Overview

Sealed Artifact 2-049 is a puppet associated with the Antigonus family. It is brought to Tingen because of its ability to sense and pursue objects connected to that bloodline.

## Behavior

The puppet appears inert but can move under supernatural conditions. People within its influence gradually become stiff and puppet-like if the proper precautions are not maintained.

## Use in Tingen

Nighthawks from another city transport 2-049 to help locate the Antigonus notebook. Its usefulness comes with strict handling procedures, constant observation, and the danger of its own hostile effects.

## Classification

Its Grade 2 designation means it is dangerous enough to cause significant harm but can still be used by trained official Beyonders under controlled conditions.
