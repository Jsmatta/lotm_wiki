## Overview

The Justiciar Pathway emphasizes authority, rules, judgment, and restricting the actions of others. Xio Derecha follows its early sequences.

## Early Sequences

- **Sequence 9: Arbiter** — physical enhancement, authority, and an instinct for law and disorder
- **Sequence 8: Sheriff** — tracking, combat improvement, and verbal commands that constrain targets

## Volume 2 Role

The pathway is common among official and military forces. Its abilities suit law enforcement, bounty hunting, and situations where controlling a conflict matters more than raw destruction.
