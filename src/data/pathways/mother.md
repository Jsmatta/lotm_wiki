## Overview

The Mother Pathway is associated with plants, harvests, medicine, biological growth, and the Earth Mother.

## Known Beyonders

Father Utravsky follows this pathway and uses its abilities in service of Harvest Church.

:::spoiler volume=3

Frank Lee demonstrates its more dangerous experimental side. His attempts to improve crops and living organisms are effective, inventive, and frequently horrifying.

:::
