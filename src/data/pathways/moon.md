## Overview

The Moon Pathway is associated with the Sanguine and appears in Volume 2 through Emlyn White.

## Volume 2 Focus

Emlyn's introduction brings vampire society and blood-related mysticism into the story. The pathway is tied to vitality, medicine, beauty, blood, and the moon.

## Early Impression

- Strong life force
- Medicine and recovery
- Blood-related abilities
- Sanguine identity and pride

## Narrative Role

The Moon Pathway adds another non-church perspective to Backlund's Beyonder world and gives the Tarot Club a member with very different priorities from Klein, Audrey, Alger, or Derrick.
