## Overview

The Mystery Pryer Pathway focuses on hidden knowledge, ritual magic, supernatural perception, and the analysis of mystical phenomena.

## Volume 1

Old Neil is a Sequence 9 Mystery Pryer and teaches Klein practical mysticism. His fate demonstrates the pathway's central danger: knowledge can observe the seeker in return.

:::spoiler volume=3

## Volume 3

Cattleya and Bernadette reveal the pathway at much higher sequences, combining occult knowledge with powerful fairy-tale-like and prophetic abilities.

:::
