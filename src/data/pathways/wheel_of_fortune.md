## Overview

The Wheel of Fortune Pathway governs luck, probability, fate, and supernatural intuition.

## Volume 2 and 3 Examples

Will Auceptin demonstrates high-level authority over fate. The Die of Probability contains power from the same pathway and turns probability itself into a dangerous environmental effect.

## Risks

Probability-based effects are difficult to predict or control. The Die of Probability shows how a high-level characteristic can turn ordinary events into lethal coincidences.
