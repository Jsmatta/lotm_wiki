## Overview

The Door Pathway governs travel, escape, spatial movement, and the recording of supernatural powers. Fors Wall follows this pathway.

## Known Volume 2 Sequences

### Sequence 9: Apprentice

Apprentices possess strong spiritual intuition and can pass through obstacles or escape restrictive spaces.

### Sequence 8: Trickmaster

Trickmasters gain a collection of practical spells used for deception, movement, and survival.

## Risks

The Abraham family associated with this pathway suffers a dangerous curse connected to the full moon.
