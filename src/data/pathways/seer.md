## Overview

The Seer Pathway specializes in divination, spiritual perception, ritual work, subtle control over the body, performance, and identity. Volume 1 reveals its first two sequences.

## Sequence 9: Seer

Seers gain enhanced spirituality and aptitude for many forms of divination.

- Spirit vision
- Dream and pendulum divination
- Ritual magic
- Intuition for danger
- Broad knowledge of mysticism

Their answers are symbolic rather than perfectly literal, so interpretation and careful questioning remain essential.

## Sequence 8: Clown

Clowns gain extraordinary control over their bodies and expressions.

- Enhanced balance, agility, and coordination
- Precise muscle and facial control
- Improved combat movement
- The ability to keep smiling or control visible emotion under pressure

## Acting Method

The potion is digested by understanding and acting according to its name without losing one's true identity. Klein develops his Seer acting principles through work at Tingen's Divination Club.

## Volume 2 Progression

In Backlund, Klein obtains the formulas for later Seer pathway sequences and advances further while living as Sherlock Moriarty.

## Sequence 7: Magician

Magicians specialize in preparation, misdirection, and staged miracles. Their abilities reward planning and performance rather than straightforward confrontation.

- Paper figurine substitution
- Damage transfer and escape tricks
- Flame control and mobility tricks
- Stronger use of ritual tools and prepared effects

Klein digests the potion by treating every major action as a carefully prepared performance.

## Sequence 6: Faceless

Faceless Beyonders can alter their appearance and body shape, allowing them to assume new identities with far greater precision.

- Change facial features and build
- Build convincing identities
- Hide from mundane recognition
- Deepen the pathway's theme of performance and self-control

Klein's promotion lets him prepare the Gehrman Sparrow identity before leaving Backlund.
