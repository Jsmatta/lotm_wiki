## Overview

The Son of Chaos is an Outer Deity related to the Black Emperor and Justiciar pathways, deeply connected to the Nation of Disorder.

## Volume 8 Role

As the barrier weakens, the Son of Chaos exerts influence on Earth, acting as one of the major external threats seeking to reclaim His lost authority and Sefirah.
