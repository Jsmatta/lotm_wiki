## Overview

The Lord of Storms is an orthodox deity associated with seas, storms, lightning, and authority.

## Church

His church is especially influential among sailors and in coastal territories. The Mandated Punishers serve as its official Beyonder force, and the Sailor Pathway is closely associated with its clergy.

:::spoiler volume=3

## Volume 3

The church dominates official supernatural affairs in the Rorsted Archipelago. Its archbishop, Jahn Kottman, suppresses Sea God Kalvetua and investigates Gehrman Sparrow's activities.

:::
