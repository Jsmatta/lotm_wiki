## Overview

God Almighty is a Great Old One and a pillar of the universe, encompassing the Visionary, Sun, Tyrant, White Tower, and Hanged Man pathways, connected to the Chaos Sea.

## Volume 8 Role

The revival of God Almighty is a central conflict as Adam merges with the True Creator, aiming to reclaim the authority of this ancient pillar.
