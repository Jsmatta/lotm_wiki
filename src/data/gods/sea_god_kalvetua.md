## Overview

Kalvetua is a giant sea serpent worshipped as the Sea God by indigenous believers in the Rorsted Archipelago.

## Decline

By Volume 3, Kalvetua is injured, unstable, and close to death. Its madness threatens the islands through storms, earthquakes, and attempts to seize a replacement body.

## Death and Succession

The Church of the Lord of Storms destroys Kalvetua. Klein obtains the Sea God Scepter and begins answering prayers from above the gray fog, causing believers to continue receiving responses under the same title.
