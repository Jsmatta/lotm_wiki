## Overview

Lilith is the ancient Sanguine ancestor and the hidden identity behind the Earth Mother faith.

## Volume 6 Role

Emlyn receives clues connecting Lilith, the Earth Mother, and The Fool. This changes how the Sanguines understand their place in the divine order.
