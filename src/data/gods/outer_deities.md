## Overview

The Outer Deities are cosmic entities outside Earth whose pressure defines the apocalypse-level stakes of the setting.

## Volume 7 Role

Klein learns more about the Outer Deities from the Seven Lights and later revelations. Their existence reframes the gods' conflicts as preparation for a larger external threat.

:::spoiler volume=8

## Volume 8 Role

Klein's final commission to the Tarot Club is partly about preparing Earth for the coming Outer Deity crisis.

:::
