## Overview

The Mother Tree of Desire is a corrupting existence associated with uncontrolled desire, reproduction, curses, and the Rose School of Thought.

## Volume 3 Role

Its influence reaches Oravi Island through a secretive sect and the Proliferation Necklace. Cynthia is transformed after praying for help with her relationship to Amyrius Rieveldt while Klein is impersonating him.

The entity notices something unusual about Klein and attempts to exploit that connection. Even indirect attention from it is mentally and physically dangerous.
