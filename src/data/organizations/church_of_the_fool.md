## Overview

The Church of the Fool is the faith built around The Fool's miracles and the salvation of the City of Silver.

## Volume 7 Role

After the City of Silver and Moon City reach Bayam, worship of The Fool becomes a public and organized force rather than only a secret Tarot Club identity.
