## Overview

The Sanguines are a long-lived supernatural race commonly called vampires by outsiders. They are closely associated with the Moon Pathway.

## Volume 2 Role

Emlyn White introduces their culture, pride, medical knowledge, and internal hierarchy. Elder Nibbs Odora directs Emlyn to pray to The Fool, leading to Emlyn's entry into the Tarot Club.

## Culture

Sanguines see themselves as a distinguished race rather than monsters. They maintain family networks, preserve formulas and ingredients, and generally avoid unnecessary exposure to human society.
