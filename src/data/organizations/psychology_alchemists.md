## Overview

The Psychology Alchemists are a secret organization specializing in the Spectator Pathway and research into the mind.

## Volume 2 Role

Audrey Hall receives instruction from Escalante and is gradually invited into the organization. This gives her formulas, acting guidance, and access to psychological research while also placing her near a group with concealed goals.

## Methods

Members value observation, emotional influence, dreams, and the collective subconscious. Their polite social surface hides significant power over thought and behavior.
