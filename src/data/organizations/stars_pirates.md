## Overview

The Stars Pirates are the crew of Admiral of Stars Cattleya. Their flagship is the Future.

## Volume 3 Role

Klein travels with the crew through the Sea of Ruins while searching for mermaids. The voyage introduces Frank Lee and places Klein near high-level Mystery Pryer knowledge.

## Character

The crew combines experienced pirates, unusual Beyonders, and researchers. Cattleya's authority keeps the ship functional despite personalities and experiments that would destabilize a less disciplined group.
