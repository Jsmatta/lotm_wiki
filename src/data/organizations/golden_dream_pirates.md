## Overview

The Golden Dream Pirates are the crew of Vice Admiral Iceberg Edwina Edwards.

## Culture

Unlike a stereotypical pirate crew, members receive regular lessons in history, language, and mysticism from their captain. They remain dangerous criminals, but the ship operates with discipline and a strong internal identity.

## Volume 3 Role

Danitz serves as fourth boatswain. Klein cooperates with Edwina and later boards the Golden Dream during the crisis involving Groselle's Travels.
