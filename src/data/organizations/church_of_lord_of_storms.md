## Overview

The Church of the Lord of Storms is an orthodox church with major influence over sailors, ports, and coastal regions.

## Official Beyonders

Its enforcement teams are known as the Mandated Punishers. They primarily use the Sailor Pathway and respond aggressively to pirates, cults, and supernatural disasters at sea.

:::spoiler volume=3

## Volume 3

The church is the dominant orthodox power in the Rorsted Archipelago. Archbishop Jahn Kottman destroys the failing Sea God Kalvetua and later investigates the major supernatural battle near Bayam.

:::
