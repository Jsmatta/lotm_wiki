## Overview

MI9 is a Loen Kingdom intelligence organization. It appears in Volume 2 through Backlund's conflicts between official forces and foreign intelligence.

## Volume 2 Role

Ian Wright's commission draws Sherlock Moriarty into a struggle involving MI9 and Intis intelligence. The case shows Klein that Backlund's mysteries are not limited to cults or rogue Beyonders; state organizations also compete in the shadows.

## Importance

MI9 helps establish Backlund as a political battlefield. Information, artifacts, identities, and unofficial agents all matter as much as direct combat.
